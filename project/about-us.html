About GlobalDev Roadmap

GlobalDev Roadmap is a community-driven learning platform designed to help South African developers navigate the path from local fundamentals to world-class engineering roles. We curate zero-cost and low-cost international resources while optimising for the realities of South African connectivity and infrastructure.

Our Mission

Empower developers in Soshanguve and beyond by providing lean, practical guidance that is globally relevant and locally accessible.
